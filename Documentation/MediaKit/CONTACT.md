## Short message

> Hi [name] — I would like to suggest an open-source macOS project for your roundup: ShengJi.
>
> ShengJi turns microphones, media files, and sound playing on a Mac into editable text and floating captions. It also includes transcript editing, optional on-device translation, and subtitle export. Recognition audio and imported transcripts are not uploaded by the app.
>
> GitHub: https://github.com/maddylaneeee/ShengJi
>
> It supports Apple silicon and macOS 15.5 or later; Apple SpeechAnalyzer and floating live captions require macOS 26. The current public build is not Developer ID signed or notarized, so the first launch requires an explicit approval in Privacy & Security.
>
> If it fits your audience, I would be happy to provide screenshots and test material for an independent evaluation. Please decide based on your own experience with the app.
